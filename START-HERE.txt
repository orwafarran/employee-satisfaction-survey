============================================================
 EMPLOYEE SATISFACTION SURVEY  —  HOW TO START (Windows)
============================================================

You only need to do STEP 1 one time. After that, it is just
double-click and go.

------------------------------------------------------------
 STEP 1  (one time only):  Install Node.js
------------------------------------------------------------
 1. Go to:   https://nodejs.org
 2. Click the big green button that says "LTS".
 3. Open the file it downloads and click Next, Next, Install.
 (If Node.js is already installed, skip this step.)

------------------------------------------------------------
 STEP 2:  Start the app
------------------------------------------------------------
 Double-click the file named:   start.bat
 A black window opens. The FIRST time it may take a few
 minutes to get ready. Keep that black window open.
 Your dashboard opens automatically in the browser.

------------------------------------------------------------
 STEP 3:  Create your login (first time only)
------------------------------------------------------------
 The dashboard asks you to create your admin login.
 Type your email and a password (8+ characters). Done.
 Next time, you just sign in with the same email + password.

------------------------------------------------------------
 STEP 4:  Send the survey to your staff
------------------------------------------------------------
 The black window shows a line like:
      STAFF — email them this survey link:
          http://192.168.x.x:3000/
 Copy that link (or click "Copy survey link" in the
 dashboard) and paste it into a normal Outlook email to
 your staff. When they open it, they answer the survey.

------------------------------------------------------------
 WATCHING THE RESULTS
------------------------------------------------------------
 The dashboard updates by itself as people answer.
 It starts empty and fills in live. Charts, scores and the
 overall % all calculate automatically. Use the Excel / PDF
 buttons to download a report any time.

------------------------------------------------------------
 TO STOP THE APP
------------------------------------------------------------
 Just close the black window.
 (While collecting answers, leave the PC on and the window
  open, otherwise staff cannot reach the link.)

------------------------------------------------------------
 IMPORTANT
------------------------------------------------------------
 * Staff must be on the SAME office network/Wi-Fi as this PC
   to open the link. (If some staff are remote, tell Orwa and
   we will host it online instead.)
 * Answers are anonymous — no names are ever collected.
